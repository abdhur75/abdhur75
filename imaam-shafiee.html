Kulliyathul Imaam Shafiee: A Beacon of Knowledge and Achievement in Islamic Higher Education

By Abdhur Rahman Nasir | October 7, 2025

Growing up in Sri Lanka, my educational journey was deeply influenced by Islamic institutions that blended faith with academic excellence. Among them, Kulliyathul Imaam Shafiee stands out as a pillar of Islamic higher education. Founded with the vision to nurture scholars grounded in the Shafi'i school of thought, this academy has become a hub for intellectual and spiritual growth.

Located in the heart of Sri Lanka, Kulliyathul Imaam Shafiee offers comprehensive programs in Islamic jurisprudence, theology, and Arabic language. Its curriculum, inspired by Imam Shafi'i's teachings, emphasizes critical thinking alongside traditional studies. As a former student, I witnessed how the institution fosters a holistic approach, preparing graduates for roles in education, community leadership, and beyond.

The academy's achievements are remarkable. It has produced numerous scholars who contribute to global Islamic discourse. Recent alumni have pursued advanced degrees abroad, including in Pakistan where I now study. The institution's emphasis on research and interfaith dialogue has earned it recognition, promoting peace and understanding in a diverse society.
